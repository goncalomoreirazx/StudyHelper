Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our tutoring services.

How much does tutoring cost?

+

Our tutoring rates vary depending on the subject, level, and tutor expertise. We offer packages starting from $40/hour with discounts available for package bookings. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How are your tutors selected?

+

All our tutors go through a rigorous selection process including background checks, credential verification, subject knowledge tests, and teaching demonstration. We only hire the top 10% of applicants.

Can I change my tutor if it's not a good fit?

+

Absolutely! We want you to have the perfect learning experience. If you're not satisfied with your tutor, we'll match you with a different one at no extra cost.

How long are the tutoring sessions?

+

Our standard sessions are 60 minutes, but we offer flexibility with 30-minute and 90-minute options as well, depending on the student's needs and attention span.
